Paintr

Paintr is a bot that generates images based on a prompt. Use the command /paint followed by a description of the image you want to generate to get started.

In addition to generating images, Paintr can also upscale images by a factor of 8 for images under 200x200 and by a factor of 4 for images under 650x650, with the command /upscale and /light-upscale respectively.
