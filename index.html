Welcome to Paintr

Paintr is an AI image generator that can create images based on your descriptions. Simply use the command /paint followed by your desired image description, and our AI will generate a unique image for you.

In addition to image generation, Paintr can also upscale your images. Use the command /upscale followed by your image to upscale it by a factor of x8, as long as the image is smaller than 200x200 pixels.

For larger images, use the command /light-upscale followed by your image to upscale it by a factor of x4, as long as the image is smaller than 650x650 pixels.
